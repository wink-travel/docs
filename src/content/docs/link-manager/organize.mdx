To manage your WinkLinks tagss, navigate to `WinkLinks` in the top navigation bar and click on the `Tags` tab.

As your content grows, you will want to start organizing some it. We created `tags` for this purpose. You can group content using as many tags as you want.

## Create tag

<Steps>
1. Create a new tag by clicking the `Add a new tag` button.
2. Enter the name of your new tag. *e.g. Top coffee shops in Berlin*
3. Click the `Save` link.
</Steps>

## Update tag

<Steps>
1. Start by clicking the `Edit` link next to the tag you want to modify.
2. Update the name of your tag. *e.g. Best coffee shops in Berlin*
3. Click the `Save` link.
</Steps>

## Remove tag

:::note
When removing a tag, you will also remove it from all associated content.
:::

<Steps>
1. Click the `Remove` link next to the tag you want to remove.
</Steps>
