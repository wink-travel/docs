Wink rewards you with loyalty points (tokens) on qualifying transactions. Points are earned automatically and accumulate in your account over time.

## How points are earned

Every qualifying transaction on Wink earns you loyalty tokens based on the transaction amount. The platform calculates your tokens using a points percentage applied to the internal price of each transaction.

For example, if the points percentage is 2% and your booking total is $500, you earn tokens equivalent to $10 in loyalty value.

## Points percentage

The points percentage determines how many tokens you earn per transaction. This rate is set by the platform and may vary based on your subscription tier, promotional periods, or account status.

## Viewing your points

You can view your accumulated loyalty points in your account dashboard. Your points balance updates automatically after each qualifying transaction is processed.

## Using your points

Loyalty tokens can be applied toward future transactions on the platform, reducing your out-of-pocket cost. Details on redemption options are available in your account settings.
