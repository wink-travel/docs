A Wink user can choose to let you manage one of their property, affiliate or travel agent accounts. When that happens, you will receive an e-mail with an invitation request.

import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

To see your current Wink invites, follow these steps:

<Steps>
 1. Log into [my.wink.travel](https://my.wink.travel).
 2. Click on `Invites` from the left sidebar.
 3. You will see a list of all current invites.
 4. This section will list all your applications.
 5. Choose to `Accept` or `Reject` the invitation.
 6. If you accepted the invite, the account will show in the `Accounts` section.
</Steps>
