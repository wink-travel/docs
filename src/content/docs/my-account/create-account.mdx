Wink connects you with travel inventory worldwide. This article steps you through how to create your first account on Wink.

<Steps>
1. Go to [my.wink.travel](https://my.wink.travel).
2. Log in by clicking on `Sign-in / Register` in the top-right corner.
3. Once you are logged in, click on `Accounts` in the left sidebar menu.
4. Click the `Create` button to start creating a new account.
5. First choose the type of account you want to create.
6. **Name** The name of your account. *This does not need to be your real name but will be something users see when looking at your WinkLinks page or when suppliers want to connect with you directly.*
7. **Entity type** Whether you are a company or individual. *Mostly used to determine how you are eligible to receive funds.*
8. **Type** Choose a type that applies best to your account. *e.g. Influencer*
9. **Description** Write a description for your account. *This will be seen on your WinkLinks page and when suppliers want to connect with you directly.*
10. **Website** Enter your main website. *This can be your IG account URL or company website. It should be the place you do most of your online work. Suppliers will go to these links when they determine whether to connect with you.*
11. **City** Where are you based?
12. Accept our Terms & Conditions and Payment Terms.
13. Click the `Save` button.
14. You will be redirected to the `Manage account` page where you can add more details to your account.
</Steps>
