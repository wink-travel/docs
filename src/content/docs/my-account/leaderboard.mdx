The leaderboard shows how your property ranks compared to others on the Wink platform.

## Geographic rankings

Rankings are available at four geographic levels:

- **City** — How you rank in your city.
- **Country** — How you rank in your country.
- **Continent** — How you rank on your continent.
- **Global** — How you rank worldwide.

## Filtering

Use the city search dropdown to change the geographic focus. Rankings update automatically based on the selected location. Rankings are currency-specific, reflecting performance in the selected market.
