Follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. Go to [my.wink.travel](https://my.wink.travel)
2. Click on `Sign-In / Register` in the top-right corner
3. You will be taken to a login form. Click on the `Register` link below the form fields.
4. Complete the registration form and click `Register`. You are redirected to the log in page.
5. Upon login, you will be redirected to My Account again where you can create your first account.
</Steps>

Creating a Wink user account gives you access to all the features on the platform. You can create unlimited properties and affiliate accounts.
