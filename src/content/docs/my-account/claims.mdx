*Approx. time to complete: ~2 mins*

Other members can also [invite your property](/studio/search) to join Wink. You'll receive an email invite from us with a `lead` request you can claim and use to finalize property creation.

To see your current Wink claims, follow these steps:

<Steps>
 1. Log into [my.wink.travel](https://my.wink.travel).
 2. Click on `Claims` from the left sidebar.
 3. You will see a list of all current claims.
 4. Choose the claim you wish to work on and complete the missing form fields to create your account.
</Steps>
