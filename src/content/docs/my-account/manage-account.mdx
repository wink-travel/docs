Once you have created an account, here are the things you can further do to it while you are in [my.wink.travel](https://my.wink.travel).

- Update account representative
- Update account information
- Update unique system IDs *ways find your account throughout the system*
- Update address
- Update profile picture *[shown in WinkLinks and when connecting with properties and affiliates on Wink]*
- Update logo *[shown in e-mails]*

You can also...

- Manage your [online presence](/my-account/online-presence).
- Assign a [dedicated agency representative](/my-account/agency-representation) to manage your account [for a fee].
- Assign [managers](/my-account/managers) that you want to have access to your account.
- Manage [customizations](/my-account/customizations) to help you control the user experience when viewing your inventory.
- Manage [applications](/my-account/applications) to give you access to Wink as a developer and to add / extend your own features to Wink.
- Manage [webhooks](/my-account/webhooks) to get notified of Wink platform events as they occur in real-time. *E.g. Get notified of a new booking*.
