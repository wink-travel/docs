Everyone should fill out their online presence. Online presence lets users find hotels and hotels find affiliates.

<Steps>
1. **Type** Enter type of online presence. *e.g. Facebook*
2. **Account** Enter the URL to your account. *e.g. https://facebook.com/wink2travel*
3. **Audience size** Enter your audience size. *e.g. This can be your followers or the number of employees*
4. **Audience type** Enter the type of audience you have. *e.g. Family because your travel blog focuses on family travel*
</Steps>
