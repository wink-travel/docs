If you want to update your user profile for your Wink user account, follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. Go to [my.wink.travel](https://my.wink.travel).
2. Log in by clicking on `Sign-in / Register` in the top-right corner.
3. Once you are logged in, click on your profile icon, in the top-right corner, and a menu will appear.
4. Click the `Profile` link.
5. Follow the instructions to update your `e-mail`, `first` and `last name`.
6. You can also upload a new `profile picture` of yourself.
7. Click `Save`.
</Steps>

<Steps>
1. You must be logged into one of our websites.
2. Click your profile icon, in the top-right corner and a menu will appear.
3. Click the `Profile` link.
4. Follow the instructions to update your `e-mail`, `first` and `last name`.
5. You can also upload a new `profile picture` of yourself.
6. Click `Update`.
</Steps>

:::note
You new profile picture will display the next time you log out completely from Wink.
:::
