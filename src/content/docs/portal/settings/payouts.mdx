To manage your bank accounts, click on `Account > Bank Accounts` from the sidebar.

## Adding a bank account

Click `Add bank account` to register a new account. The form adapts based on your selected country:

- **Payment country** — The country where your bank is located.
- **Currency** — The currency you want to receive payouts in.
- **Account holder name** — The name on the bank account.
- **Routing details** — Country-specific fields such as Routing number (US), Sort code (UK), SWIFT/BIC code, or IBAN.
- **Account number** — Your bank account number.

## Primary account

You can designate one bank account as **Primary**. Your primary account receives all automatic payouts. Additional accounts can be used for manual withdrawals.

To change your primary account, click `Make primary` on any account in your list.

## Managing accounts

From the bank account list you can:

- Edit account details.
- Delete an account you no longer need.
- Switch which account is primary.
