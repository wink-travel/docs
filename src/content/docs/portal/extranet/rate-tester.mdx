The rate tester lets you verify that your room rates are correctly configured before they go live to travelers.

To access the rate tester, click on `Monetize > Rate Tester` from the main nav bar.

## How it works

Enter a search scenario and the rate tester will show you exactly how rates are calculated:

- **Sales channel** — Select the channel to test (e.g., your booking engine, an affiliate, or a specific integration).
- **Check-in / Check-out dates** — The travel dates to test.
- **Room configuration** — Number of adults and children.

Click `Search` to see the results.

## Results

The results page shows the calculated rates for each room type and rate plan that matches your search criteria. Use this to verify that:

- Pricing modifiers (single occupancy, extra adults/children) are applied correctly.
- Cancellation policies display as expected.
- Restrictions are filtering correctly.
- Promotions and discounts are taking effect.
