The reviews page shows all guest reviews submitted for your property.

To view your reviews, click on `Distribution > Reviews` from the main nav bar.

## Browsing reviews

The review list is filterable to help you find specific feedback:

- **Date range** — Filter by when the review was submitted.
- **Guest name** — Search by first or last name.
- **Guest email** — Look up reviews by email address.
- **Responded** — Filter by whether you have responded to the review.

Results are paginated with 25 reviews per page.

## Review details

Click any review to see the full details, including the guest's written feedback and ratings. Use this to understand guest sentiment and identify areas for improvement.
