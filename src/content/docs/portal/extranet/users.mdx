The users page lets you manage who has access to your property on the Extranet.

To manage your team, click on `Property > Users` from the main nav bar.

## Inviting team members

Enter an email address and click `Invite` to send an invitation. The recipient will receive an email with a link to join your property team.

## Managing roles

Each team member can have one of the following roles:

- **Owner** — Full access to all property settings, including the ability to manage other users.
- **Manager** — Access to day-to-day property management features.

You can promote a manager to owner using the `Make owner` button.

## Removing members

Click `Remove` next to any team member to revoke their access. This takes effect immediately.

## Pending invitations

The invitations section shows all outstanding invitations that have not yet been accepted.
