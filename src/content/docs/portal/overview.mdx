[Portal](https://app.wink.travel) is the unified web application where every section of the Wink ecosystem now lives. Previously, each feature area (Extranet, Studio, Travel Agent, Payment, Social, Link Manager) had its own URL. As of this release, they are all sections of a single Portal under `app.wink.travel`, organized as sub-paths.

## Sections

- **`/extranet`** — Property features for hotels, chains, brands, and management companies.
- **`/studio`** — Affiliate features for promoting travel inventory and earning commissions.
- **`/travel-agent`** — Travel-agent features for registered agents to search, book, and earn.
- **`/payment`** — Payment features for managing payouts and withdrawals.
- **`/social`** — Automated Social for AI-driven social media posting.
- **`/link-manager`** — Link Manager for authoring WinkLinks pages.
- **`/corporate`** — Corporate (coming soon).
- **`/settings`** — Per-account settings: branding, managers, applications, webhooks, payouts, and more.
- **`/plan`** — Manage your Wink subscription.
- **`/invite`** — Accept invites to manage other accounts.
- **`/analytics`** — Performance dashboards across your accounts.
- **`/leaderboard`** — See how your property ranks geographically.
- **`/claims`** — Turn property leads into full listings.

## Portal vs. User Settings

Portal handles everything you do as an organization or account holder. Personal user-profile management (email, password, passkeys, OAuth app consents) lives separately on the **IAM** service at `iam.wink.travel/my-account`. See [User Settings](/account/overview) for more.

## Switching accounts

You can manage multiple accounts — properties, affiliate accounts, travel-agent accounts, corporate accounts — from a single login. Use the account switcher in the top navigation to move between them. Settings, analytics, and most features scope per-account.
