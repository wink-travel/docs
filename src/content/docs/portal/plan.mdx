The subscription page lets you manage your plan and the properties licensed under your account.

## Available plans

Browse available subscription tiers displayed via our pricing table. Each plan includes different feature sets and property limits. Select the plan that best fits your needs.

## Current subscription

If you already have an active subscription, this section shows your current plan details and status.

## Licenses

The license table shows all properties provisioned under your subscription. You can:

- View which properties are currently licensed.
- Add new property licenses using the `Add license` button.
- Manage billing through the Stripe customer portal link.

:::note
Billing management (payment methods, invoices, plan changes) is handled through the Stripe customer portal.
:::
