The Travel Agent dashboard provides a quick overview of your performance and activity.

## Metrics

The dashboard shows key metrics at a glance:

- **Bookings** — Total bookings you have facilitated.
- **Revenue** — Total revenue generated through your bookings.
- **Upcoming bookings** — Bookings with future check-in dates.

## Activity

The activity section tracks your recent booking history and trends over time, helping you identify peak periods and top-performing properties.

## Account status

The dashboard shows the current state of your travel agent profile — whether setup is complete and commissions are active.
