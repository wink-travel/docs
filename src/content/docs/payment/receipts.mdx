Each booking on TripPay generates a receipt that you can access from the booking details page.

## What's on a receipt

A receipt includes:

- **Payment details** — Method of payment, transaction IDs, and timestamps.
- **Amount breakdown** — Room charges, ancillary items, and totals.
- **Cancellation policy** — The policy that applied at the time of booking.
- **Refund information** — Any refunds processed, with amounts and dates.

## Receipt types

TripPay provides two receipt formats:

- **Guest-friendly TripPay receipt** — A clean, branded receipt suitable for sharing with the guest.
- **Payment gateway receipt** — The full transaction record from the payment processor.

Both are accessible via links on the booking details page.
