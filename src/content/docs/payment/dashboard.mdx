The TripPay dashboard gives you a real-time snapshot of your financial activity.

## Overview

The top of the dashboard shows key metrics at a glance:

- **Bookings** — Total number of bookings processed.
- **Total revenue** — Aggregate revenue across all bookings.
- **Your earnings** — Your share of the total revenue after fees and commissions.
- **Upcoming bookings** — Number of future bookings.
- **Guest checked-in** — Guests currently on premises.
- **Funds available** — Amount ready for withdrawal.

## Booking funds flow

The pipeline view shows the real-time status of funds at each stage of the booking lifecycle — from initial payment through settlement and payout. Use it to track where money sits at any point in time.

## Insights

The insights section provides analytics on booking trends, cancellation rates, and revenue over time so you can spot patterns and plan ahead.
