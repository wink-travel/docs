To view your booking history, click on `Bookings` from the sidebar.

## Booking grid

The booking grid displays all your transactions in a filterable, sortable table. You can:

- Filter by **date range** (defaults to the last 30 days).
- Sort by date, amount, or guest name.
- Page through results.

Each row shows the traveler name, property, guest count, stay dates, and total amount.

## Booking details

Click any booking to see its full details:

### General information

- Booking ID, creation date, payment method, and payment status.

### Breakdown

- Room type, occupancy (adults and children), and stay dates.
- Ancillary items — any extras purchased along with the room.

### Fund breakdown

Shows exactly how funds are distributed across all parties:

- **Commission** — Affiliate or agent commission.
- **Platform fee** — TripPay processing fee.
- **Hotel agency fee** / **Affiliate agency fee** — If applicable.
- **Sale amount** — Net amount to the property.

### Payment information

- Transaction IDs (TripPay, Merchant, Trace ID).
- Links to the **guest-friendly TripPay receipt** and the **payment gateway receipt**.
- External booking code and supplier identifier, if available.

### Refunds

If a refund has been processed, you will see the refund amount, date, cancellation reason, and who initiated the cancellation.

### Payout

Payout method (e.g., Virtual Credit Card) and issue date.
