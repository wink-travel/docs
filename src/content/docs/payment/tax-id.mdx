Some regions require a national tax identifier before you can receive payouts. TripPay will prompt you to complete this step as part of your account setup.

## Submitting your tax ID

When prompted, enter your national tax identifier:

- **Individuals in the U.S.** — Your Social Security Number (SSN). *Format: 111-11-1111*
- **Companies in the U.S.** — Your Employer Identification Number (EIN). *Format: 11-111111*
- **Other regions** — Your country's equivalent national identifier.

After entering your tax ID, you will be asked to confirm that the information is correct before submitting.

:::note
This is a one-time step required for KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance. You may be contacted for additional documentation depending on your region.
:::
