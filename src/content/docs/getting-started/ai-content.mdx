Wink uses artificial intelligence to help you create polished, multilingual content for every part of your travel inventory. Whether you're registering a new property or refreshing an existing listing, AI accelerates your time-to-market from weeks to minutes.

## How it works

Wink's content engine is built around a resilient multi-provider AI architecture. When you request AI-generated content, the platform automatically selects the best available provider from a pool of leading AI services — including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and others. If one provider is temporarily unavailable, the system seamlessly falls back to the next, ensuring you never experience downtime.

## What AI generates

AI content generation is available across **9 inventory types**:

| Inventory type | What AI produces |
|---|---|
| **Property** | Welcome text, descriptions, highlights |
| **Guest rooms** | Room descriptions, features, selling points |
| **Restaurants** | Dining descriptions, cuisine highlights, ambiance |
| **Spas** | Treatment descriptions, wellness highlights |
| **Activities** | Experience descriptions, itinerary narratives |
| **Attractions** | Attraction overviews, historical context |
| **Meeting rooms** | Space descriptions, event capabilities |
| **Places** | Point-of-interest descriptions, local context |
| **Add-ons** | Package and bundle descriptions |

## Tone of voice

You can customize the tone of AI-generated content to match your brand. Available tones include:

- **Neutral** — Straightforward and informative.
- **Friendly** — Warm and approachable.
- **Luxury** — Refined and upscale.
- **Foodie** — Culinary-focused and taste-driven.
- **Adventurous** — Bold and action-oriented.
- **Family** — Welcoming for families with children.
- **Romantic** — Intimate and couple-friendly.
- **Humorous** — Light-hearted and playful.
- **Eco** — Sustainability and nature-focused.
- **Local insider** — Like advice from a local expert.
- **Inclusive** — Accessible and welcoming to all.
- **Budget** — Value-conscious and deal-oriented.

Choose the tone that best fits your property's identity and target audience.

## AI-assisted registration

The most powerful use of AI on Wink is during property registration. Simply provide your property's website URL and Wink will:

1. **Scrape your website** to understand your property.
2. **Generate descriptions** — welcome text, property highlights, and summaries.
3. **Suggest amenities** — mapped from your website content to industry-standard amenity codes.
4. **Extract demographics** — location details, property type, and audience.

You review and refine the results, then publish. What used to take weeks is done in under an hour.
