You are already connected to WinkLinks, our link manager tool. Connect to more of your social networks networks by clicking on `Connect a social network`.
The process will redirect you to your social network to approve the `Wink Social`app to post content on your behalf.

Once you are connected, we start generating content for that network for the next scheduled month.

:::note
We create and publish content optimized for each social network.
:::
