We show you a dashboard that aggregates analytics from across all your social networks and tracks how your content performs.

## Insights

The insights dashboard gives you a high-level view of engagement across all your connected social platforms:

- **Views** — Total post impressions across all networks.
- **Shares** — Number of times your posts were shared.
- **Clicks** — Number of link clicks from your posts.
- **Click rate** — Percentage of views that resulted in a click.
- **Share rate** — Percentage of views that resulted in a share.

## Activity

The activity section provides two trend views over time:

- **Views & Clicks** — See how your visibility and engagement trend together.
- **Click & Share rate** — Track your engagement rates to understand which content resonates most.

## Per-platform analytics

You can drill down into analytics for each connected social network individually:

- **Facebook** — Page-level metrics for your connected Facebook Business Page.
- **Instagram** — Account-level metrics for your connected Instagram Business Account.
- **WinkLinks** — Metrics for posts published to your WinkLinks page.

For greater insight into whether posts converted into bookings, go to [my.wink.travel](https://my.wink.travel) and head to the Analytics section.
