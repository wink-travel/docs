The Studio dashboard gives you a quick overview of your affiliate performance.

## Activity

The activity section shows your booking analytics — how many bookings you have driven, revenue generated, and conversion trends over time.

## Supplier search

Use the quick search to find suppliers by property name. This is a shortcut to browsing the supplier network.

## Account status

The dashboard shows the current status of your account — whether your profile is complete and ready to earn commissions.

## Leaderboard

See how you rank against other affiliates based on performance metrics.
