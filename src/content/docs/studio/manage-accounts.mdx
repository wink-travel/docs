Wink Studio is located at [https://studio.wink.travel](https://studio.wink.travel). Once you are authenticated, you will be redirected to our [accounts page](https://studio.wink.travel/company-list). If this is your first time, we recommend reading through how to get set up with your first affiliate account. Keep on reading. Otherwise, you can read more about how you can customize your account.

## Create account

Click the `Create new account` button to continue. You will be redirected to our [account creation](https://studio.wink.travel/company/create) page. 

Fill in all required fields.

- ***Name*** The name of your account. *This does not need to be your real name but will be something users see when looking at your WinkLinks page or when suppliers want to connect with you directly.*
- ***Entity type*** Whether you are a company or individual. *Mostly used to determine how you are eligible to receive funds.*
- ***Type*** Choose a type that applies best to your account. *e.g. Influencer* 
- ***Description*** Write a description for your account. *This will be seen on your WinkLinks page and when suppliers want to connect with you directly.*
- ***Website*** Enter your main website. *This can be your IG account URL or company website. It should be the place you do most of your online work. Suppliers will go to these links when they determine whether to connect with you.*
- ***City*** Where are you based?
- ***Agreement*** Accept our [affiliate agreement](/studio/terms-of-service) and [payment terms](/studio/payment-terms) to continue.

Once created, you can customize your account further by filling in your address and adding a profile picture for your affiliate account. 

<Steps>
1. Click on your new affiliate account. *This will take you to your account dashboard.*
2. Click on `Account > Profile` in the top navigation bar.
3. On the Profile page, you will be able to update your existing profile and add additional information to it.
</Steps>

:::note
Your affiliate account profile picture should not be confused with your Wink user profile picture. Your affiliate account profile picture will be displayed on your WinkLinks page and seen by suppliers lookig to connect with you. 

Your user profile picture is mostly for internal use.
:::
