You can create a user account through any of our portals. Once created, this account can be used across all of Wink's apps and websites.

## Travelers

Follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. Head on over to [https://ota.wink.travel](https://ota.wink.travel)
2. Click on `Sign-In / Register` in the top-right corner
3. You will be asked to enter basic user details. Fill in all required fields and click `Save`.
4. You can now log into Wink with your username (e-mail) and password.
5. Once logged in, the system redirects you back to where you came from.
</Steps>

## Registration

Creating a Wink user account gives you access to all the features on the platform. You can create unlimited properties and affiliate accounts.

<Steps>
1. The first time you are directed to our authorization server, you'll be asked to enter your username and password.
2. Instead, click on `Create account` button.
3. Fill out all required fields and click `Register` to continue. This takes you back to the sign-in form.
4. Enter your e-mail / username and password to log in.
5. You are redirected back to the site you came from.
</Steps>
