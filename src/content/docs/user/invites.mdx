A Wink user can choose to let you manage one of their property, affiliate or travel agent accounts. When that happens, you will receive an e-mail with an invitation request. 

import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

To see your current Wink invites, follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. You must be logged into one of our websites.
2. Click your profile icon, in the top-right corner and a menu will appear.
3. Click the `Invites` link.
4. Interact with your invites by either `Accepting` or `Rejecting` them.
</Steps>
