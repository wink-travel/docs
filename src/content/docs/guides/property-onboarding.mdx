Getting your property live on Wink involves several steps. This guide gives you a clear checklist so nothing gets missed.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- A Wink account. [Create one here](/my-account/create-account) if you don't have one.
- Photos of your property, rooms, and facilities.
- Your rate and availability information.
- Your cancellation and payment policies.

## The onboarding journey

<Steps>
1. **Create your property** — Go to https://extranet.wink.travel and register your property. The onboarding wizard will guide you through the basics: property name, type, address, and contact details.

2. **Complete your profile** — Add your property description, social media links, and general manager information. A complete profile builds trust with travelers and affiliates. See [Profile](/extranet/profile).

3. **Upload photos and videos** — Add high-quality images for your property and each room type. Properties with good photos convert significantly better. See [Photos & Videos](/extranet/photos-videos).

4. **Set up your guest rooms** — Create room types with bed configurations, max occupancy, and room-specific amenities. See [Guest Rooms](/extranet/guest-rooms).

5. **Add amenities** — Select the amenities your property offers (Wi-Fi, parking, pool, etc.). These are used for search filtering and display. See [Amenities](/extranet/amenities).

6. **Configure rate plans** — Set up at least one rate plan with a cancellation policy and any meal inclusions. See [Rate Plans](/extranet/rate-plans).

7. **Set master rates** — Enter your daily prices for each room type and rate plan combination. See [Master Rates](/extranet/master-rates).

8. **Add your policies** — Define your house policies (check-in/out times, pet policy, smoking, etc.). See [Policies](/extranet/policies).

9. **Set up a sales channel** — Activate the Wink Network to make your property visible to affiliates, or create direct channels for specific partners.

10. **Map your location** — Pin your property on the map so it appears in location-based searches. See [Map Location](/extranet/map-location).

11. **Review your dashboard** — Check the [Dashboard](/extranet/dashboard) for profile suggestions. The progress ring shows your completion percentage.
</Steps>

## Optional but recommended

These steps are not required to go live but will improve your visibility and conversion:

- **Add facilities** — Restaurants, spas, meeting rooms, activities, and attractions. Each becomes a bookable or browsable item. See [Restaurants](/extranet/restaurants), [Spas](/extranet/spas), [Meeting Rooms](/extranet/meeting-rooms).
- **Add welcome text** — A personalized message from the general manager. See [Welcome Text](/extranet/welcome-text).
- **Complete the Green Index** — Answer sustainability questions to earn a green score. See [Green Index](/extranet/green-index).
- **Add recognition** — Awards, certifications, and badges. See [Recognition](/extranet/recognition).
- **Enable CalDAV sync** — Keep your external calendar in sync. See [Syncing Your Calendar with CalDAV](/guides/syncing-calendars-with-caldav).

## Going live

Once your profile, rooms, rates, and at least one sales channel are configured, your property is live. Travelers can find and book you through the Wink booking engine, affiliate links, and partner channels.

:::tip
Use the [Rate Tester](/extranet/rate-tester) to verify your rates are displaying correctly before sharing your property link.
:::
