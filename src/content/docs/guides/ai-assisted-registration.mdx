Wink's AI-assisted registration lets you onboard a property in under an hour. Instead of manually filling in every description and amenity, you provide your property's website URL and AI does the heavy lifting — generating welcome text, descriptions, amenity suggestions, and demographic information.

## Prerequisites

- A Wink account. See [Create an Account](/my-account/create-account).
- Your property's website URL (the more detailed the website, the better the AI results).

## How it works

<Steps>
1. **Navigate to the Extranet** and start creating a new property.

2. **Enter your property's website URL** when prompted. Wink's web scraper visits your site and extracts information about your property — including descriptions, images, amenities, and location details.

3. **AI generates your content.** Based on the scraped data, AI produces:
   - **Welcome text** — A polished introduction to your property.
   - **Property descriptions** — Highlights and summaries for your listing.
   - **Amenity suggestions** — Facilities and services detected on your website, mapped to industry-standard codes.
   - **Demographics** — Property type, location details, and target audience.

4. **Review the AI output.** Everything AI generates is a suggestion. Review each section and edit as needed. You have full control over the final content.

5. **Choose your tone of voice.** If you'd like a different style, select a tone (e.g., Luxury, Friendly, Adventurous) and regenerate. See [AI-Powered Content](/getting-started/ai-content) for all available tones.

6. **Save and continue** with the rest of your property setup — rooms, rates, photos, and more.
</Steps>

## Tips for best results

- **Provide a content-rich website.** The more information on your website (descriptions, amenity lists, location details), the better AI can generate accurate content.
- **Use your primary language.** Enter your website in your primary language. Wink can translate the AI-generated content into 40+ languages afterward.
- **Review amenity mappings.** AI maps your amenities to standardized codes used across booking platforms. Double-check that the mappings are correct.
- **Regenerate if needed.** Not happy with the output? Try a different tone of voice or regenerate individual sections.

## What happens next

After AI generates your initial content, you can:

- **Edit any section** directly in the Extranet.
- **Add photos and videos** to complement the AI-generated text.
- **Set up rooms and rates** to start receiving bookings.
- **Translate** your content into additional languages.
