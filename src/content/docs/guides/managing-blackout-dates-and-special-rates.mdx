Not every day is priced the same. Events, holidays, off-peak seasons, and last-minute gaps all call for different pricing. This guide shows you how to use special rates and rate plan restrictions to handle these scenarios.

## Event pricing

When a conference, festival, or local event drives demand, you'll want to increase rates for those dates.

<Steps>
1. Go to `Monetize > Special Rates` in your Extranet.
2. Click `Create a new special rate`.
3. Select the master rate and room type to override.
4. Set the date range to cover the event period.
5. Enter the adjusted (higher) daily prices.
6. Click `Save`.
</Steps>

The special rate overrides the master rate for those dates only. Your regular pricing remains unchanged outside the event window.

## Seasonal adjustments

For longer periods (high season, shoulder season, low season), you can either:

- **Use special rates** — Create overrides for each season with adjusted pricing.
- **Update master rates** — Adjust the base rates directly for those periods.

Special rates are better for temporary adjustments, while updating master rates makes sense for predictable annual patterns.

## Last-minute rates

To fill unsold rooms close to the check-in date:

<Steps>
1. Create a new **rate plan** with an advance booking restriction (e.g., must book no more than 3 days in advance).
2. Pair it with a **special rate** offering a discount for the target dates.
3. This rate plan will only appear to travelers booking within the last-minute window.
</Steps>

## Blackout dates

To block certain dates from a specific rate plan (e.g., no promotional rates during peak season):

<Steps>
1. Go to `Monetize > Rate Plans` and select the rate plan.
2. Navigate to the **Restrictions** section.
3. Add a **Sell date** restriction to limit when the rate is bookable.
4. Or use **Stay date** restrictions to limit when the rate covers.
</Steps>

For example, you can restrict your "Early Bird" rate plan so it's not available for bookings during December (peak season), while your regular rate plan remains open.

## Combining restrictions

Restrictions can be stacked for precise control:

| Restriction | What it controls |
| -- | -- |
| **Sell dates** | When the rate can be booked |
| **Stay dates** | Which stay dates the rate covers |
| **Available days** | Which days of the week the rate appears |
| **Arrival days** | Which days the guest must arrive on |
| **Departure days** | Which days the guest must depart on |
| **Required days** | The guest must stay over at least one of these days |
| **Advance booking** | How far in advance the booking must be made |
| **Length of stay** | Minimum and maximum nights |
