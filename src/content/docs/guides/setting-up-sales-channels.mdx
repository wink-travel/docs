Sales channels are how you control the distribution of your inventory. A sales channel defines who can sell your rooms, what commission they earn, and which rate plans are available.

## Types of sales channels

There are three main ways to distribute your property on Wink:

- **Wink Network** — Your property is visible to all affiliates on the platform. This is the broadest distribution.
- **Direct channels** — You create a private channel for a specific affiliate or partner with a custom commission rate.
- **Your own website** — Use shareable links or embedded cards to sell directly from your site with no intermediary.

## Creating a sales channel

<Steps>
1. Go to https://extranet.wink.travel and select your property.
2. Navigate to the sales channel section.
3. Click `Create a new sales channel`.
4. Give the channel a name (e.g., "Website Direct", "Partner - Kim Travel").
5. Set the **commission percentage** — how much the affiliate earns per booking.
6. Select which **rate plans** are available on this channel.
7. Optionally restrict to specific **room types** or **inventory**.
8. Click `Save`.
</Steps>

## Wink Network

The Wink Network is the default distribution channel. When activated, your property appears in [Studio](/studio/search) where any affiliate can find and sell it.

To activate:

<Steps>
1. Go to your property's network settings.
2. Enable `Wink Network`.
3. Set a default commission rate for network affiliates.
</Steps>

:::tip
The Wink Network is the easiest way to get broad exposure. You can always create direct channels later for your top-performing partners with different commission rates.
:::

## Direct affiliate channels

For affiliates you want to work with closely, create a direct channel with a custom commission.

<Steps>
1. Find the affiliate through the Network section. See [Network](/extranet/network).
2. Create a direct sales channel for that affiliate.
3. Set a custom commission rate (which may be higher or lower than your network default).
4. Select which inventory is available on this channel.
</Steps>

This is useful when you want to offer an influencer or travel agent a preferred rate or exclusive access to certain rooms.

## Commission structure

Commissions are expressed as a percentage of the booking total. The commission is split among the parties involved:

- **Affiliate commission** — What the seller (affiliate, travel agent, influencer) earns.
- **Platform fee** — Wink's processing fee.
- **Property revenue** — The remainder goes to you.

You control the affiliate commission. The platform fee is deducted automatically.

## Monitoring performance

Once your channels are live, use [Analytics](/my-account/analytics) to track which channels drive the most bookings and revenue. This helps you optimize commission rates and decide which partnerships to invest in.
