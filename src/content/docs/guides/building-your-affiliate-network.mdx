Affiliates — influencers, travel agents, bloggers, and booking sites — sell your rooms to their audiences and earn a commission. This guide goes beyond [Finding an Influencer](/guides/finding%20an-influencer) and covers the full lifecycle of building and managing your affiliate partnerships.

## How affiliates work on Wink

1. An affiliate finds your property on [Wink Studio](/studio/search).
2. They create a shareable link, card, or embed for your property.
3. A traveler clicks the link and books.
4. The affiliate earns a commission on the booking.
5. You receive the booking minus the commission and platform fee.

## Step 1 — Make your property discoverable

Before affiliates can sell your property, they need to find it.

<Steps>
1. Activate the **Wink Network** on your property so it appears in affiliate search results.
2. Complete your property profile — properties with photos, descriptions, and competitive rates attract more affiliates.
3. Set a competitive **default commission rate** for network affiliates.
</Steps>

## Step 2 — Find and invite partners

You can also proactively seek out partners:

<Steps>
1. Go to the **Network** section in your Extranet. See [Network](/extranet/network).
2. Browse affiliates by category, location, or audience type.
3. Send an invitation to affiliates you want to work with.
4. Offer them a direct channel with a custom commission if they're a good fit.
</Steps>

## Step 3 — Create direct channels for top partners

For affiliates who drive significant volume or have a strategic audience, create a direct sales channel:

<Steps>
1. Navigate to sales channels for your property.
2. Create a new channel named after the partner (e.g., "Partner - Kim Travel").
3. Set a custom commission rate — this can be higher than your network default to incentivize the partnership.
4. Optionally restrict which room types or rate plans are available on this channel.
</Steps>

## Step 4 — Track and optimize

Use analytics to monitor which affiliates perform best:

- Which partners drive the most bookings?
- What's the average booking value per partner?
- Which channels have the highest conversion rate?

Based on this data, you can adjust commission rates, create exclusive offers for top performers, or phase out underperforming channels.

## Tips for success

- **Competitive commission** — A higher commission attracts more and better affiliates. Consider your total cost of acquisition vs. other distribution channels.
- **Respond to requests** — When an affiliate sends you a request, respond quickly. First impressions matter.
- **Provide great content** — Properties with quality photos, detailed descriptions, and complete amenity listings give affiliates better material to work with.
- **Exclusive offers** — Consider giving your top affiliates access to exclusive rate plans or early access to promotions.
