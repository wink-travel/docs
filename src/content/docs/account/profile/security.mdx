The **Security** tab at [iam.wink.travel/my-account/security](https://iam.wink.travel/my-account/security) is where you manage **passkeys** — WebAuthn credentials that let you sign in without a password.

## What is a passkey?

A passkey is a cryptographic credential bound to your device (or password manager) that proves you are you. Passkeys are phishing-resistant and faster than typing a password.

## Registering a passkey

1. Sign in to [iam.wink.travel](https://iam.wink.travel) with your existing credentials.
2. Go to **My Account → Security**.
3. Click `Add passkey`.
4. Your browser will prompt you to authenticate with your device (Touch ID, Face ID, Windows Hello, a hardware key, or a synced password manager).
5. Give the passkey a friendly name (e.g. *Work MacBook*) so you can recognize it later.

## Using a passkey to sign in

On the [iam.wink.travel](https://iam.wink.travel) sign-in screen, enter your email — if you have a passkey registered for that email, you'll be prompted to authenticate with it instead of typing a password.

## Removing a passkey

In the same Security tab, each registered passkey has a `Remove` button. Use it if you lose a device, retire a hardware key, or want to clean up unused credentials.

:::caution
Removing all your passkeys leaves password as the only sign-in method. Make sure you remember your password before removing the last passkey.
:::
