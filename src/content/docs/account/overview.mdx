[Wink IAM](https://iam.wink.travel) is the identity service for your Wink **user account** — distinct from the **organization accounts** (properties, affiliate accounts, etc.) that you manage in [Portal](/portal/overview).

## What lives here

- **`/my-account/general`** — Your user profile: first name, last name, username, email, profile picture, password.
- **`/my-account/security`** — Passkeys (WebAuthn) for password-less sign-in.
- **`/my-account/apps`** — OAuth2 applications you've authorized to access your Wink account. Revoke access at any time.

## User Settings vs. Portal: what's the difference?

| Concept | Lives on | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **User identity** (you, the human) | `iam.wink.travel` | Email, password, passkeys, profile photo |
| **Organization accounts** (properties, affiliate orgs, etc. that you operate) | `app.wink.travel` | Branding, managers, payouts, applications, webhooks |

One user can operate many organization accounts; one organization account can be operated by many users (via [managers](/portal/settings/managers)).

## Sign-in flow

Sign-in always starts at IAM. Once authenticated, you're routed into Portal, where you pick which organization account you want to work in.
