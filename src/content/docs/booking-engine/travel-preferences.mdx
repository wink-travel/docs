While on https://ota.wink.travel, you can complete your `travel profile` that lets hotels and experience provider know more about you.

A travel profile contains:

- Basic information.
- Locale preferences.
- Emergency contacts.
- Address.
- Personal information.
- Pets.
- Physical challenges.
- Hotel preferences.
- Amenity preferences.
- Safety preferences.
- Corporate preferences.
- Disability preferences.
- Room preferences.
- Room amenity preferences.
- Personal preferences.

At the bottom of your profile, you're asked if you want to share this profile with hotels you visit. If you select to share, the hotel will see the full profile in their booking confirmation.
