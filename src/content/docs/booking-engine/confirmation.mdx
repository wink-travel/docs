After a successful payment, the traveler lands on the booking confirmation page.

## Thank you page

The confirmation page shows:

- A thank-you message confirming the booking.
- The **booking code** for reference.
- A note that a confirmation email has been sent.

If the booking is still being processed, a brief "Confirming your booking..." message is displayed until the server confirms.

## Booking details

Once confirmed, the traveler can view the full booking details:

- Hotel name and location.
- Room type and configuration.
- Check-in and check-out dates.
- Guest information.
- Price breakdown.
- Cancellation policy.
- Payout and payment method details.

## Guest actions

From the booking details page, authenticated travelers can:

- **Cancel the booking** — If the cancellation policy allows it.
- **Leave a review** — Rate and review the property.
- **Send a message** — Contact the hotel directly.
- **Share** — Share the booking on social media.
- **Print** — Print the booking confirmation.

Unauthenticated guests see a read-only view of their booking details.
