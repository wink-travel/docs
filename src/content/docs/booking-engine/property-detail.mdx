When a traveler clicks on a property from the search results, they land on the property detail page.

## Overview

The detail page shows everything a traveler needs to make a booking decision:

- **Hero image** — The property's featured photo.
- **Description** — Localized property description.
- **Photo and video gallery** — Browse images with a full-screen slideshow.
- **Star rating and guest scores** — Overall rating and review summary.
- **Location** — Interactive map showing the property's location.

## Room types

Available room types are displayed with their best price. Clicking a room type opens the **room configurator** where the traveler can:

- View room specifications (beds, bathroom, view, style).
- Browse room-specific amenities.
- Select bed configuration and preferences.
- Add ancillary offerings (breakfast, spa packages, etc.).
- See real-time pricing updates.

## Amenities

Amenities are organized by category:

- Hotel amenities (services, security, accessibility).
- Room amenities.
- Restaurant amenities.
- Spa amenities.
- Meeting room amenities.

## Additional sections

Depending on what the property has configured, travelers may also see:

- **Restaurants** — On-site dining options.
- **Spas** — Spa services and treatments.
- **Activities** — Available activities and experiences.
- **Attractions** — Nearby attractions.
- **Meeting rooms** — Conference and event facilities.
- **Announcements** — Special promotions or notices.
- **Guest reviews** — Reviews from past guests.
- **Recognition and green index** — Sustainability and award badges.
