The checkout process guides travelers from room selection through payment in three steps.

## Step 1 — Room configurator

After selecting a room type on the property page, the room configurator opens. Here the traveler can:

- Choose bed configuration and room preferences.
- Add ancillary items (breakfast, spa, activities, etc.).
- Enter guest information.
- Review the price breakdown (net and gross totals).
- Add the room to their shopping cart.

## Step 2 — Shopping cart

The shopping cart shows all items ready for purchase:

- Room type, dates, and guest count for each item.
- Cancellation policy for each booking.
- Per-item and total price breakdown.

Travelers can remove items or proceed to payment.

## Step 3 — Payment

The payment step embeds a secure payment widget where the traveler enters card details and completes the transaction. After a successful payment, the traveler is redirected to the confirmation page.

If payment fails, an error message is displayed and the traveler can retry.
